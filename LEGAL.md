# Legal and removal requests

This repository contains open-source client integration code. It does not host media obtained from supported websites and is not affiliated with or endorsed by those websites.

## Voluntary site-owner removal

A verified website operator or authorized representative may request removal of an extension for their website without submitting a formal DMCA notice. Open a private security advisory in this repository and include:

- the website and extension involved;
- your name, role, and authority to act;
- a reliable way to verify control of or representation for the website;
- the requested scope and relevant URLs.

After reasonable verification, maintainers may remove the extension source, repository listing, and distributed APK and prevent its reintroduction. Voluntary removal is not an admission of infringement or wrongdoing.

Do not place personal information, legal notices, or confidential evidence in a public issue.

## Copyright, trademark, privacy, and other legal requests

These are distinct categories and may require different information. GitHub-hosted content can also be reported through GitHub's applicable content-removal process. If GitHub forwards a formal notice, the maintainers will follow GitHub's process and applicable law.

The maintainers may temporarily unpublish affected artifacts while reviewing a credible request. A removed source must not be re-added unless the request is withdrawn, authorization is established, or the dispute is otherwise resolved.

This policy describes the repository's operational process and is not legal advice.
